**SUBHARTI UNIVERSITY — COMPREHENSIVE FACTUAL CONTEXT**

**IDENTITY**
- **Official Name**: Swami Vivekanand Subharti University (SVSU)
- **Type**: Private, multidisciplinary university
- **Establishment**: 2008 (Subharti University Act, 2008)
- **Founder**: Dr. Atul Krishna (Surgeon, Philanthropist, Visionary)
- **Founding Trust**: MTV (Mahanayam Theravadam Vajrayanam) Religious and Charitable Trust
- **Location**: Subhartipuram, NH-58, Delhi–Haridwar Bypass Road, Meerut, Uttar Pradesh
- **Region**: Delhi-NCR, approx. 65 km from Delhi, 10 km from Meerut city center
- **Campus Size**: 250 acres (lush green)
- **Medium of Instruction**: Primary – English; some programs offered in Hindi or Bilingual mode
- **Core Vision/Philosophy**: "Education for All" with emphasis on quality, moral values, social responsibility, and nation-building.

**ADMISSION PROCESS**
- **Process Start**: Generally April/May (varies by program).
- **Application Modes**:
    - Online: Official website (www.subharti.org)
    - Offline: Admission Cell on campus.
    - Contact: +91-963-901-0567, +91-963-922-2288/89/90/91/93/98.
- **Admission Criteria**:
    - Entrance Exams: NEET (MBBS/BDS/MD/MS), Subharti National Eligibility Test (SNET).
    - Merit-based: Qualifying exam marks, spot test, interview.
- **Required Documents**: 10th/12th mark sheets & certificates, Graduation marksheets (PG), Transfer/Character Certificate, Passport photos, Aadhaar, Domicile, APAAR/ABC ID, Entrance scorecard (if applicable).
- **International Students**: Welcome; support for visa, accommodation, FRRO/Form C formalities. Process: Eligibility check → Offer Letter → Visa Offer Letter → Post-arrival registration.

**FEES & FINANCIAL SUPPORT**

**HOSTELS & ACCOMMODATION**
- **Total Hostels**: 16 (including 2 NRI hostels). Separate for Boys & Girls.
- **Room Types**: Single and shared accommodation available.
- **Amenities**: Wi-Fi, 24/7 power & water, mess, indoor games, laundry services.
- **Allotment**: First-come, first-served basis upon payment of hostel fee.
- **Outside Stay**: Permitted (student's choice), though hostel stay is recommended.

**CAMPUS FACILITIES & SERVICES**
- **Healthcare**:

- **Sports**: Olympic-size stadium, indoor complexes, gymnasiums, yoga, SPA.
- **Auditoriums**: Maangalya Convention Center (2500 capacity), others.
- **ICT**: Wi-Fi enabled campus, leased line >20 Mbps, smart classrooms.
- **Banking**: Punjab National Bank branch & ATMs on campus.
- **Food**: Multiple multi-cuisine canteens and cafeterias.
- **Transport**: University bus fleet for commuting within ~60 km (Meerut, Muzaffarnagar, etc.).
